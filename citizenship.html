I do believe that honest, compassion, respect, responsibility, and courage are necessare components of good citizenship.

*Citizenship: behaving in a way that respects you and the people around you. Also following the rules or laws in given place.

*Honesty: being truthful to yourself and others, holding yourself accountable for actions.

*Compassion: having pride and love for what you are representing or what you are doing.

*Respect: caring or admiring a certain person or object for their accomplishments or actions.

*Responsibility: having control over certain actions and being held accountable for said actions.

*Courage: being brave, taking control and doing something that scares many who are not willing to take a risk.

*Good Morals: knowing what is right vs. wrong and knowing how to fix your mistakes. This ties back into responsibilities.
